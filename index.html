How to Play

Plan your route before you drive

Start in Georgia with 3,596 points. Pick only the yellow-highlighted connected states, keep an eye on your fuel, and hit the goal before the tank runs dry.

  • Each stop adds that state's points and uses 1 fuel.
  • Louisiana, Arkansas, and Tennessee give bonus fuel.
  • Use the map or the Next Stops cards to move.
  • Your difficulty level changes your fuel and target score.
